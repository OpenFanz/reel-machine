# Legal / brand-safe + the teardown template

## Use the reference for FORMAT, not the file
You are cloning the *mechanic* of a viral reel, not republishing it.
- **Study the structure**, then rebuild it with YOUR model, YOUR angle, YOUR own or licensed audio.
- **Do NOT** copy the person's face, handle, or exact spoken lines.
- **Do NOT** re-upload the source file. If you ever show the reference on screen, keep it a short snippet, mute the original audio, and blur the face/handle — framed as format analysis.
- Use a synthetic/AI face for your model; don't impersonate a real person. Use a voice you have rights to.
